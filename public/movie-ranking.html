The Best Movies According to Ketan

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Godfather

A gripping portrayal of the Mafia underworld, showcasing power, family, and betrayal.

The Shawshank Redemption

A story of hope, friendship, and redemption set within the harsh confines of a prison.

The Dark Knight

A thrilling, dark, and complex superhero film that redefined the genre with its compelling portrayal of heroism and villainy..
